Leave Email Format for Office

Writing a leave email is important when you want to inform your manager about your absence. A proper format ensures clarity and professionalism.

Format

  • Subject: Leave Request
  • Greeting
  • Reason for leave
  • Duration
  • Closing

Example

Subject: Leave Request
Dear Manager,
I would like to request leave from 26th to 30th April to attend a family function.
Thank you.
