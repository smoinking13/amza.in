What Can You Do with AMZA Tools?

AMZA Tools gives you three handy utilities in one place — completely free, with no sign-up required. Everything runs inside your browser, so your files stay on your device and are never sent to any server.

🖼️ Image to PDF

Turn one or more photos into a single PDF in seconds. Great for combining scanned pages, submitting assignments, or creating a photo album to share.

How to use: Drag your images into the upload box → click Convert to PDF → your PDF downloads automatically.

📄 PDF to Images

Need just one page from a long PDF? Convert each page into a high-quality PNG image and save only the ones you need. Useful for presentations, social posts, or sharing specific slides.

How to use: Upload your PDF → click Convert Pages to Images → preview all pages and download individually.

🔍 Image to Text (OCR)

Have a screenshot, photo of a document, or a scanned page with text you need to copy? OCR reads the text from your image and gives you editable, copyable content instantly.

How to use: Upload an image → click Extract Text → copy or edit the recognised text.

🔒 Your Privacy Matters

All conversions happen directly in your browser — nothing is uploaded to our servers. Your files are never stored, shared, or seen by anyone else. No account is needed. It is safe to use even for sensitive documents like contracts, medical records, or personal notes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?
Yes. All three tools are free with a daily limit of 5 conversions to keep the service running smoothly for everyone.

Do I need to create an account?
No sign-up, no email — just open the page and start converting.

Will my file quality be reduced?
Images are scaled proportionally and PDFs are rendered at high resolution, so quality is well preserved in normal use.

Does OCR work on handwritten text?
OCR works best on clearly printed text. Handwriting can be recognised but accuracy depends on how legible the writing is.

Can I use it on my phone?
Yes, AMZA Tools is fully mobile-friendly. You can upload images from your phone's camera roll and download results directly.
